Security on the Internet

Nowadays, almost all of us use a computer or mobile devices. We browse the internet, download files, send e-mails. One of the most important issues that concerns us is online security. Security is not only protecting our devices against viruses, but also protecting our data. In this article, we'll take a look at how antivirus software works and what steps we can take to increase our online security.


How does antivirus software work?

Antivirus software is a program that protects our computer or mobile device from viruses, Trojans and other harmful programs. These types of programs work by analyzing files and monitoring the behavior of the operating system in order to detect potentially dangerous files.

Antiviruses protect us in several different ways. First, they scan our files and check for viruses. Secondly, they record suspicious system activities, such as attempts to modify the system registry or create new files without our knowledge. When the antivirus detects suspicious behavior or a virus on the system, it launches the appropriate security mechanisms. Once a threat is detected, the antivirus can isolate the infection or remove it from the system.


What are the types of antiviruses?

There are three main types of antiviruses:

Single anti-virus software - these are programs that we install on a single device. This solution is often used for home computers.

Antivirus software in the network - is a solution used to protect entire corporate networks or computer networks in schools or other institutions. In this case, the antivirus works centrally on the server.

Antivirus software in the cloud - this is the latest solution that is gaining popularity. Cloud antivirus works over the internet and you have access to antivirus tools without having to install them on your device. In this way, we can gain more flexibility and convenience of use.



Is it worth buying an antivirus?

Computer viruses are one of the biggest problems related to online security, and their number is growing every year. Their popularity is primarily due to their effectiveness, as well as the popularity of the Internet, which allows them to spread quickly. Files attached to e-mails and numerous websites that use insecure communication protocols also play a large role in the spread of viruses.


How to take care of your online security?

In addition to installing antivirus software on your device, we have several other tools at our disposal to help keep us safe online. Here are some basic rules:

Avoid malicious websites and files - online safety starts with us. Never download files from unknown sources and never visit suspicious websites.

Let's collect information about viruses - the more we know about online threats, the better we will be able to protect our devices against malware. You can watch YouTube videos on this topic.

Regularly update the software - always keep the software on our device up to date. Software development companies, including antivirus developers, release regular updates to close vulnerabilities and remove new threats.
